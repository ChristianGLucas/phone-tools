`libphonenumber-js` is a zero-dependency rewrite of Google's
[libphonenumber](https://github.com/google/libphonenumber) (Apache-2.0). The
region metadata it bundles is derived from that project. This package imports
`libphonenumber-js/max` (full metadata, for accurate number-type detection) and
`libphonenumber-js/mobile/examples` (example numbers for CountryInfo).

The remaining runtime modules (`@grpc/*`, `@opentelemetry/*`, `protobufjs`,
`google-protobuf`, and their transitive dependencies) are the Axiom TypeScript
service scaffolding generated by `axiom init`; they are licensed under
Apache-2.0, MIT, BSD-3-Clause, BSD-2-Clause, or ISC. A full-tree scan found no
copyleft (GPL/AGPL/LGPL/MPL) license anywhere in the dependency graph.
